About me

I'm known as Bryn Refill around the Internet. I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer and System Engineering and I worked for an IT company, where I was involved in microservices-oriented software development.

At the moment, I'm pursuing a Master's degree in the same field with an emphasis on cybersecurity and I'm focused on experimenting with side projects and solving CTF challenges in my spare time.

My current goal is to become a cybersecurity engineer and advance my career in the industry.
